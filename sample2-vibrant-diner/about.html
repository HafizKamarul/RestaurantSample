How It All Started

Urban Pizza Co. was born from a simple dream: to bring the authentic taste of Italian wood-fired pizza to the bustling streets of Kuala Lumpur. In 2022, our founder Marco Antonio, a third-generation pizzaiolo from Naples, decided to share his family's 80-year-old pizza recipes with Malaysia.

After extensive research and countless recipe tests, we opened our doors with a commitment to using only the finest ingredients - from San Marzano tomatoes imported directly from Italy to locally sourced fresh herbs and vegetables.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of happy customers who have made us their go-to destination for authentic, delicious pizza in a vibrant, modern atmosphere.

Our Pizza Process

From dough to delicious - see how we create the perfect pizza

1

Fresh Dough

Our dough is made fresh daily using a 48-hour fermentation process for the perfect texture and flavor.

2

Quality Toppings

We carefully select and prepare each topping, from imported Italian cheeses to locally sourced fresh vegetables.

3

Wood-Fire Magic

Our authentic wood-fired oven reaches 900°F, creating the perfect crispy crust and smoky flavor in just 90 seconds.

4

Perfect Finish

Fresh herbs, premium oils, and final touches are added before serving your pizza hot and fresh to your table.
